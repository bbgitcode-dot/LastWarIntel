# Roadmap

> **Sentinel Development Roadmap**

Version: **v0.8.0**

---

# Philosophy

The Sentinel roadmap is not a feature backlog.

It represents the planned evolution from a data processing application into a strategic intelligence platform.

Every milestone should increase Sentinel's ability to answer one question:

> **What deserves our attention today?**

# Guiding Principles

Every roadmap item should satisfy at least one of these goals.

Increase

* strategic understanding
* explainability
* decision quality
* automation of repetitive work
* confidence in recommendations

If a feature does not improve strategic decision making, it should not be implemented.

---

# Long-Term Objective

The long-term objective is simple.

A president should be able to open Sentinel every morning and immediately understand

* what changed,
* why it matters,
* and what deserves attention today.

When Sentinel consistently provides those answers, the vision of the project will have been achieved.
