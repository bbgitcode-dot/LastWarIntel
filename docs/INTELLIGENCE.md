# Intelligence Concepts

## Fact

An objective observation.

Example:

Alliance lost 12% power.

---

## Evidence

Supporting observations.

Example:

- THP decreased
- Officers left
- Activity declined

---

## Reasoning

Interpretation based on evidence.

Example:

Alliance stability is decreasing.

---

## Recommendation

Suggested action.

Example:

Initiate diplomatic contact within 48 hours.

---

## Recruitability

Probability that players leave an alliance.

---
