# Sentinel Vision

> **Building the Strategic Intelligence Platform for Last War**

Version: **v0.8.0**

---

# Mission

Sentinel exists to transform raw game observations into **explainable strategic intelligence**.

Its purpose is not to collect data.

Its purpose is not to visualize rankings.

Its purpose is not to automate leadership.

Its purpose is to help alliance commanders make **better strategic decisions** by reducing noise, increasing understanding and highlighting what truly deserves attention.

---

# Vision

Competitive Last War has evolved beyond what manual leadership can efficiently process.

Every day generates:

* thousands of player changes
* alliance growth and decline
* whale movement
* recruitment opportunities
* strategic threats
* diplomatic opportunities
* server-wide shifts

The amount of information grows faster than any leadership team can analyze.

Sentinel closes this gap.

It transforms observations into structured knowledge and knowledge into actionable recommendations.

---

# North Star

Sentinel should eventually be able to answer questions like:

* Which alliance is most likely to collapse?
* Which whales are worth recruiting?
* Which server is becoming unstable?
* Which transfer opportunity should we pursue first?
* What changed overnight?
* What deserves the president's attention today?

Every new feature should move Sentinel closer to answering these questions.

---

# Product Principles

## Intelligence over Information

Information alone creates little value.

Knowledge creates advantage.

Sentinel always prioritizes intelligence over raw statistics.

---

## Explainability over Complexity

Every recommendation must be understandable.

Every recommendation must be reproducible.

Every recommendation must be traceable back to the original observations.

If a recommendation cannot be explained, it should not exist.

---

## Human First

Sentinel assists decision makers.

It never replaces them.

The final decision always belongs to humans.

Sentinel provides:

* evidence
* context
* assessments
* prioritization

Leadership provides:

* experience
* diplomacy
* timing
* responsibility

---

## Deterministic Intelligence

Sentinel is not a probabilistic AI.

Given identical observations, Sentinel always produces identical conclusions.

Confidence represents evidence quality.

It never represents randomness.

---

## Strategic Relevance

Every metric, indicator and assessment must answer a meaningful strategic question.

If it does not improve decision making, it does not belong inside Sentinel.

---

# What Sentinel Is

Sentinel is:

* an intelligence platform
* an expert system
* a strategic decision support system
* an explainable reasoning engine
* a knowledge platform

---

# What Sentinel Is Not

Sentinel is not:

* a dashboard
* a spreadsheet replacement
* an OCR application
* a statistics viewer
* an autonomous AI

These technologies support Sentinel.

They are not Sentinel itself.

---

# Long-Term Direction

The long-term objective is to evolve from analytics toward strategic decision support.

```text
Raw Data

↓

Knowledge

↓

Understanding

↓

Recommendations

↓

Strategic Decisions
```

Future versions will gradually expand from describing the current situation to anticipating future developments.

Examples include:

* alliance collapse prediction
* recruitment forecasting
* diplomatic recommendations
* transfer intelligence
* server stability forecasting
* multi-season trend analysis

---

# Success Criteria

Sentinel succeeds when alliance leadership spends less time searching for information and more time making decisions.

A successful Sentinel session should answer:

* What happened?
* Why did it happen?
* Why is it important?
* What should we do next?

within a few minutes.

---

# Guiding Philosophy

The goal of Sentinel is not to build the most advanced analytics platform.

The goal is to build the most useful strategic intelligence platform.

Every architectural decision, every intelligence module and every future feature should support this single objective.

---

# Vision Statement

> **Sentinel transforms observations into explainable strategic intelligence that empowers alliance leaders to make faster, better and more confident decisions.**
