# Sentinel Vision

## Mission

Sentinel transforms raw game data into explainable strategic intelligence.

Its purpose is not to display data.

Its purpose is to support better strategic decisions.

---

## Product Principles

### Intelligence over Information

Sentinel prioritizes attention, not data.

Raw information is never hidden.

Instead, Sentinel presents the most relevant intelligence first while allowing users to drill down into every underlying detail.

---

### Explainability

Every recommendation should be traceable.

Fact

↓

Evidence

↓

Reasoning

↓

Recommendation

---

### Human First

Sentinel assists commanders.

It never replaces strategic thinking.

Recommendations must always be understandable and verifiable.

---

### Data Integrity

Sentinel never invents data.

Confidence is always shown where certainty cannot be guaranteed.

---

### Long-Term Goal

Become the strategic intelligence platform for competitive server management.
